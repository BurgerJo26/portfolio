My design philosophies

Curious, methodical, and outcome-driven.

01

Asking why, then why again

Most design problems hide a deeper one. I dig until I find the actual job to be done — for both users and the business.

02

Trust is a UX feature

In regulated, high-consequence software, the interface carries trust. Every pattern, label, and transition either builds it or erodes it.

03

Design at the speed of evidence

I prototype fast and validate faster — because the cheapest way to be right is to be honestly wrong early.
